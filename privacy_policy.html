Cookies

Cookies are files with a small amount of data that are commonly used as anonymous unique identifiers. These are sent to your browser from the websites that you visit and are stored on your device's internal memory.

Our mobile Applications do not use these "cookies" explicitly. However, the apps use third-party advertising networks (AdMob) that may use cookies or similar technologies to collect information for serving personalized advertisements. You have the option to either accept or refuse these technologies and know when they are being used on your device through your device settings.
